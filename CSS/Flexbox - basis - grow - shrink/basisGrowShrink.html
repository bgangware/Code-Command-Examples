Flexbox - Basis Grow Shrink

flex-basis - 80px

flex-basis - 80px; 1st div flex-grow - 1;

!

flex-basis - 80px; 1st div flex-grow - 1; 6th div flex-grow - 1

note: equal space on both sides

!

flex-basis - 80px; 1st div flex-grow - 1; 6th div flex-grow -2

note: the unused space is allocated 2 to 1 to the right

flex-basis - 80px; all divs flex-grow - 1

note: the unused space is equally allocated amongst the divs

flex-basis - 80px; all divs flex-grow - 1; max-width - 200px

note: the unused space is equally allocated amongst the divs until the maximun width of 200px is reached

flex-basis - 600px; 3rd div flex-shrink - 1.5;

note: the 3rd div shinks much faster than the other divs

flex-basis - 600px; 4rd div flex-shrink - 0;

note: when a flex-shrink of 0 is applied the div doesn't shrink at all
